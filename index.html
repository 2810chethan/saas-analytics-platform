Understanding Cohort Retention Heatmaps

This heatmap tracks monthly customer cohorts (horizontal rows) over their subsequent lifecycle months (vertical columns). Net Revenue Retention (NRR) above 100% indicates that expansion revenue from existing accounts outpaces losses from churn.
